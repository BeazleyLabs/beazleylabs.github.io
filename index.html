What it's all about

Labs is about the freedom to experiment, make mistakes, and trial concepts that we can't always pilot within the corporate environment. We are free to 'fail fast' and therefore learn quickly, proving (or disproving) concepts rapidly and creating a much more informed business case for the ideas that prove worthy enough to be developed further. We also use the Labs banner to contribute back to the opensource community.
